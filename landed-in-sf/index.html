Landed in SF

02 October 2015

I touched down in San Francisco yesterday! The flight was 1 hour from Glasgow to Dublin then 11 hours from Dublin to SF. There was a lot of fog in Glasgow and flights were delayed, luckily I made my connection.
I got the place I was staying okay via taxi, it cost about 65 dollars to get from the airport to downtown.
I have to buy some groceries (since eating out here is going to be way too expensive) and open a US bank account today.
I'm also going to try to meet with one of the guys who is starting Hack Reactor with me so it's all go.
I have three days till Hack Reactor begins!

William Henshaw
